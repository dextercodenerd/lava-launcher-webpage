Lava Launcher

The family-friendly way to play Minecraft with mods

A new Minecraft launcher designed specifically for younger players and families, making modded Minecraft accessible to everyone regardless of technical skill.

🚧 HEAVY DEVELOPMENT: Lava Launcher is currently in pre-alpha. No playable builds are available for installation yet. This website was hastily build for review of the app's Microsoft login by Mojang. More is coming when my evenings give me some free time :)
